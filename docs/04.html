4. Tutorial 2: realistic dendriteÔÉÅ

We utilized a schematic shape of spiny dendrite in tutorial 1, but can incorporate any shape, including morphologically realistic shapes of cells. In pariticular, LD has utility functions to incorporate 3D segmentations of volumetric EM images from the software Reconstruct 1 .

LD also has importer from manual annotation from UNI-EM anntator 2.

We would like to introduce how to do it.
